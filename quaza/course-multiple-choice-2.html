SECTION IV

Choose from the alternatives lettered A to D the one which is nearest in meaning to the ud word or expression in each sentence.

31. The long strike dislocated the operations of the factory.
A, twisted
B. afflicted
C. disrupted
D. stopped

32. When he realized that he was the culprit, he absconded.
A, bolted
B. confessed
C. fainted
D. yielded

33. The President spoke of the rebels with disdain and refused to talk with them.
A, anger
B. sadness
C. contempt
D. impudence

B. reject
C. escape
D. discard

B. chastised
C. frightened
D. humiliated

B. source

D. significance

B. demand
C. appeal
D. request

A, interested
B. skilful
C. confident
D. prolific

A, negotiation
B. agreement
C. law
D. document

A, an invitation
B. a meeting
C. an appointment
